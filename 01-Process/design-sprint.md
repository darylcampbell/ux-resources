# Design Sprint

A Product Design Sprint is a technique to quickly design, prototype, and test the viability of an idea, product, or feature.

## Google Design Sprint

Based on a 5 day process covering the following:

* Unpack
* Sketch
* Decide
* Prototype
* Test
